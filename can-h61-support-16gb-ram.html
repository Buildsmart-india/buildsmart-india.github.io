Can H61 Support 16GB RAM?

Yes, most H61 motherboards support up to 16GB DDR3 RAM, but there are limitations.

Important Requirements

  • 2 RAM slots (8GB each)
  • Supported processor (2nd/3rd Gen Intel)
  • Updated BIOS version

Maximum Supported RAM Speed

Most H61 boards support DDR3 1333MHz or 1600MHz depending on CPU.

Upgrade Recommendation

If you are upgrading for gaming, pairing 16GB RAM with an SSD significantly improves performance.
