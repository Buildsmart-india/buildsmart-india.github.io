How Much PSU Wattage Do You Need?

Choosing the right PSU is critical for system stability and safety.

Basic Formula

Add CPU wattage + GPU wattage + 100W extra buffer.

Example – RX 6600 Build

  • CPU – 65W
  • GPU – 132W
  • Total with buffer – Around 350W
  • Recommended PSU – 550W 80+ Bronze

Why Not Buy Exact Wattage?

Extra wattage improves efficiency and allows future upgrades.
